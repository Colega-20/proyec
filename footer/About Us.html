Our Story

From PC enthusiasts to your trusted hardware partner

ComponentBox was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate PC enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of reliable, knowledgeable service in the PC hardware industry. We saw too many customers receiving wrong advice, incompatible parts, or paying inflated prices for components they didn't need.

What started as a small operation in Bonao, Dominican Republic, has grown into a trusted destination for PC builders across the region. Our mission has always been simple: provide authentic, high-quality PC components at fair prices, backed by expert advice and outstanding customer service.

Today, we serve over 10,000 happy customers, from first-time builders assembling their gaming rigs to professionals upgrading workstations. Every component we sell is carefully selected from authorized distributors, ensuring you receive genuine products with full manufacturer warranties.
