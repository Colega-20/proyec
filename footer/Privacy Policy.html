1. Categories of Data Collected

To process your hardware orders with maximum precision, ComponentBox collects:

  • Transaction Data: Information necessary for legal billing and tax compliance.
  • Hardware Profile: If you use our PC configurator, we save your build specifications to offer you personalized technical support and incompatibility alerts for future purchases.
  • Navigation Telemetry: IP address, browser type, and site behavior through unique identifiers to prevent fraud in high-value payments.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Purpose of Data Processing

We don't just sell hardware, we optimize your experience:

The collected data is used for warranty validation with manufacturers (such as ASUS, MSI, or NVIDIA), reverse logistics management, and sending critical updates about "product recalls" due to mass factory defects.

3. Security Infrastructure

We use end-to-end encryption via TLS 1.3. Your data is stored on redundant servers with SOC2 certifications and complies with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) standard, ensuring that your personal information is inaccessible to unauthorized third parties.

Privacy and Cookie Usage

Detailed Cookie Policy

At ComponentBox we use "cookies" to make your browsing as smooth as an NVMe SSD. These are small pieces of data that we store in your browser.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Allow you to maintain your hardware cart and active session. Session
Analytics Help us know which GPUs are most searched to adjust stock. 2 years
Customization Remember if you prefer to see prices in USD or local currency. 1 year

How to manage cookies?

You can block them in your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). Keep in mind that if you block essential cookies, you won't be able to complete the component purchase process.

Personal Data Protection

Your data is protected by international data protection laws (GDPR). We collect information only to:

  • Validate the warranty of your components with manufacturers like Intel, AMD, or EVGA.
  • Notify you about critical firmware updates for your products.
  • Prevent fraud through billing address verification.

User Rights: You have the right to request complete deletion of your data from our servers at any time by sending an email to data-protection@componentbox.com. when it is avalible.
